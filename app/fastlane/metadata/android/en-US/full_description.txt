KIAN VPN is an open-source, privacy-first client for secure access to the open internet.

Features:
• Add servers from a subscription link, QR scan, or manual entry
• Automatic best-server selection by latency
• Modern protocols (VLESS Reality, Shadowsocks, and more)
• Bilingual UI (Persian RTL + English) with dark/light mode
• No Google Play Services (GMS) dependency — works on any device
• No logs, no ads, no tracking

Your private key stays on your own device and no personal data is collected.
Full source code (MIT) is available on GitHub.
